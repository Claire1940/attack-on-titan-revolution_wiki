## The Attack Raid: Step-by-Step Strategy

The Attack Raid is a two-phase encounter that requires both team coordination and individual skill. To make the most of your **Attack on Titan Revolution emperor key**, you need to survive both phases.

### Phase 1: The Defense
In the first phase, your team is tasked with defending Eren while eliminating waves of Titans. This phase is relatively straightforward but requires speed.
*   **Focus:** Kill every Titan as quickly as possible.
*   **Priority:** Protect the central objective to ensure the mission doesn't fail early.
*   **Tip:** Use high-mobility builds to zip between Titans and keep the area clear.

### Phase 2: The Boss Fight
Once the cinematic plays, you will face off against the Attack Titan. This is where you earn your damage percentage.
*   **The Scream:** When the boss screams, back away immediately. Getting caught in the radius causes massive damage and ragdolls your character, wasting precious time.
*   **The Red Skull Icon:** Look for the Red Skull marker on the boss's body. Hitting these specific points deals critical damage and significantly boosts your damage percentage.
*   **Damage Threshold:** Aim for at least 10-15% damage to ensure you get maximum rewards, though 5% is the hard minimum for chest access.

## Utilizing Attack Shards and Resonance

Once you have used your **Attack on Titan Revolution emperor key** to open an Emperor Chest, you will likely find Attack Shards. These are not just for show; they are the primary resource for the Resonance system.

Resonance is a mechanic that boosts the power of your Attack Titan form. If you have unlocked the Attack Titan, you can navigate to your Equipment tab to view the Resonance menu. By consuming Attack Shards, you increase your Titan's stats, making future raids much easier to manage.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not spend your Attack Shards on Resonance until you have actually unlocked the Attack Titan form. Buying charges early is a waste of resources that could be spent elsewhere.
