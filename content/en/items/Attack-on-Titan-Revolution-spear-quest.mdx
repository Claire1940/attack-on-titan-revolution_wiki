## Overview of the 2.5 Update and Quest Requirements

Before diving into the specific steps of the **Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest**, it is important to understand the context of the recent 2.5 update. This patch introduced significant buffs to the Fritz family, making it one of the most powerful lineages in the game. The Fritz passive now allows players to unlock any family-exclusive shifter skill, such as the Jaeger family's Berserk mode, while also providing a "Scream" ability that can eliminate nine nearby Titans instantly.

To begin your journey for the Thunder Spears, you must navigate through three distinct phases. Each phase rewards you with a specific part of the weapon: the Handle, the Shaft, and the Explosive Tip. You cannot skip ahead; the game requires you to complete the tasks in a linear fashion to unlock the final "Defend the Supplies" mission.

## Phase 1: Building Watchtowers in the Outskirts

The first step of the **Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest** takes place in the Outskirts. Your goal is to locate specific "Green Circles" scattered across the map. These circles indicate the construction sites for new Watchtowers.

To build a tower, you simply need to stand still within the green circle. A progress bar will appear, and once it fills, the tower is complete. Interestingly, you can perform this action while in Titan form, which can help you stay protected from wandering enemies while you wait. You must complete this process three times.

Once the third tower is erected, a horse carriage will spawn nearby. This triggers an escort mission similar to the standard Outskirts game mode. You must defend the carriage from approaching Titans as it makes its way to the Wall. If you are using the Fritz family, utilizing your "Scream" in Titan form will provide a significant buff to nearby allies, making the defense much easier. Upon successful delivery, you will receive the Thunder Spear Handle.

> **💡 Tip:** Progress for building towers is shared among all party members. Splitting up to find circles can speed up the process, but stay close enough to support each other during the escort phase.

## Phase 2: Hunting Hardened Titans at Utgard Castle

After securing the handle, the next stage of the **Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest** moves to Utgard Castle (UTG). This is widely considered the most combat-intensive portion of the quest due to the introduction of a new, resilient Titan variant.

These "Hardening Titans" are easily identifiable by their shiny, metallic appearance. Unlike standard Titans, they possess significantly higher health and damage resistance. Even high-tier family skills or Titan shifter abilities may fail to kill them in a single hit.

### How to Defeat Hardened Titans

| Method | Requirement for Kill | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Blade Combat** | **3 Hits** to the Nape | Requires high precision and durability. |
| **Titan Shifter** | **3 Punches** | Standard attacks are less effective; focus on heavy hits. |
| **Fritz Auto-Kill** | **Non-Instant** | Reduces health significantly but won't one-shot. |

You need to eliminate three of these shiny Titans to progress. Because they appear frequently in Utgard Castle missions—especially in Aberrant mode—you can usually complete this requirement in a single run. Once the third Hardened Titan falls, you will earn the second part of the spear.

## Phase 3: Scavenging the Giant Forest

The third part of the **Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest** is often the most frustrating for players due to the visual difficulty of the objective. You must travel to the Giant Forest and locate small supply crates.

These crates are very small and have a faint yellow outline. Because the Giant Forest is filled with dense foliage and large trees, the crates are notoriously difficult to spot. They do not have high contrast against the ground, so you must look closely for the yellow circular glow.

1. **Locate the Crate:** Search the base of large trees and near cliff edges.
2. **Transport to Base:** Once you pick up a crate, a yellow icon will appear on your map/HUD indicating the Base Camp.
3. **Repeat:** You need to deliver three crates total to the camp.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Only about two crates spawn per game session, though this may scale with the number of players in the lobby. It is highly recommended to do this with friends so you can cover more ground and share the delivery progress.

## The Final Challenge: Defending the Supplies

Once all three parts are collected, the final mission of the **Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest** becomes available. You must participate in a "Defend the Supplies" mission. This is a wave-based survival challenge where you must prevent Titans from destroying the stockpile of newly crafted spears.

After successfully defending the supplies, the Thunder Spears will be added to your inventory. However, before you can use them effectively in high-level raids like the [Armored Titan Raid](https://www.roblox.com/games/14236083942/Attack-on-Titan-Revolution), you must visit the upgrade menu.

### Thunder Spear Mechanics

*   **Capacity:** You can carry 8 spears at a time.
*   **Refilling:** You must return to a supply crate during missions to restock once you run out.
*   **Detonation:** Currently, spears do not have a manual detonation button. They explode automatically when they impact a Titan or when you move within a certain proximity of the spear after it has lodged into a target.
*   **Damage:** They deal massive AOE (Area of Effect) damage, making them perfect for clearing groups or breaking Titan armor.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I complete the Attack on Titan Revolution spear quest solo?**

A: While it is possible to complete the quest solo, it is significantly harder, especially during the Giant Forest scavenging phase and the Hardened Titan hunt. Grouping up allows you to share progress on watchtower building and crate collection.

**Q: Why can't I see the quest for the second part of the spear?**

A: The quest stages are strictly sequential. You must complete the Outskirts watchtower and escort mission first. Once the "Handle" is in your inventory, the Utgard Castle requirements will unlock in your quest log.

**Q: Do Thunder Spears replace my primary blades?**

A: No, Thunder Spears act as a secondary gear item. You will still use your blades for standard mobility and nape strikes, while the spears serve as high-damage projectiles for specific situations.

**Q: Are the Hardened Titans found in every map?**

A: No, for the purposes of the quest, you should focus on Utgard Castle. They are specific spawns that look "shiny" or metallic compared to the standard skin texture of regular Titans.
