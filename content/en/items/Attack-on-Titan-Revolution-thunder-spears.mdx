## Phase 1: Building Watchtowers in the Outskirts

The journey to obtain the **Attack on Titan Revolution thunder spears** begins in the Outskirts. This first phase focuses on construction and defense, requiring players to interact with the environment in a way that differs from standard combat missions.

### How to Build Watchtowers
Once you load into the Outskirts map, look for glowing **green circles** scattered across the terrain. To begin building a watchtower, you must stand perfectly still within the circle. A progress bar will appear, indicating the tower's construction. 

> **💡 Tip:** You can actually build these towers while in Titan form! This is often safer as it allows you to swat away any smaller Titans that might interrupt your progress.

### The Escort Mission
After successfully building three watchtowers, a horse carriage will spawn. This triggers an escort phase similar to the standard Outskirts missions. You must defend the carriage from oncoming Titan waves as it makes its way toward the wall. If the carriage is destroyed, you will have to restart the phase. Successfully reaching the wall rewards you with the **Thunder Spear Handle**, the first of three essential components.

## Phase 2: Hunting Hardened Titans in Utgard

With the handle in your possession, the next step takes you to Utgard. This phase is significantly more difficult than the first because it introduces a new enemy type: the **Hardened Titan**. These Titans are visually distinct, featuring a shiny, metallic-looking skin that indicates their high defense.

To complete this segment, you must eliminate **three Hardened Titans**. Note that these enemies are incredibly resilient; standard attacks that would normally kill an Aberrant will barely scratch them.

| Attack Type | Requirement to Kill Hardened Titan |
|---|---|
| **Blades** | Requires **3 precise hits** to the nape. |
| **Titan Form** | Requires **3 heavy punches** to the nape/head area. |
| **Fritz Instakill** | Does **NOT** instantly kill these Titans; only deals high damage. |

Because these Titans appear frequently in Utgard (especially on Aberrant difficulty), you can usually complete this requirement in a single match. Once the third Hardened Titan falls, you will receive the second component of the **Attack on Titan Revolution thunder spears**.

## Phase 3: The Scavenger Hunt in the Giant Forest

The final component is hidden within the Giant Forest. Many players find this to be the most frustrating part of the quest because the items are small and difficult to spot against the dense foliage. You are looking for **small crates** outlined with a faint **yellow circle**.

### Finding the Crates
These crates do not have high contrast, making them blend into the shadows of the trees. There are typically two crates available per session, though this number may scale based on the number of players in your lobby. 

1. **Locate the crate:** Scan the base of large trees and bushes.
2. **Carry to Base:** Once you pick up a crate, a yellow icon will appear on your map/HUD indicating the base camp.
3. **Delivery:** Drop the crate at the designated spot in the camp.

> **Warning:** You are vulnerable while carrying crates. Ensure your teammates provide cover, or clear the immediate area of Titans before attempting the delivery.

Once you and your squad have delivered three crates total (progress is shared), the final quest "Defend the Supplies" will unlock. Completing this final defense mission grants you the full **Attack on Titan Revolution thunder spears** gear.

## How to Use Thunder Spears Effectively

Now that you have the gear, you need to understand its mechanics. Unlike some other Roblox games where explosives can be detonated remotely, the **Attack on Titan Revolution thunder spears** require specific proximity and management.

### Combat Mechanics
*   **Proximity Detonation:** You cannot manually explode the spears from a distance. You must fly in close to the Titan's nape or limbs to trigger the explosion.
*   **Ammo Capacity:** You start with a limited supply (usually **8 spears**). Once these are exhausted, you must return to a supply crate to refill.
*   **Upgrades:** It is highly recommended to visit the upgrade tree immediately. Increasing the blast radius and reload speed makes a world of difference in high-level raids.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Thunder Spears while in Titan Form?**

A: No, the **Attack on Titan Revolution thunder spears** are an equipment item for your human scout form. However, having a teammate in Titan form is helpful for defending you while you use them.

**Q: Why can't I see the crates in the Giant Forest?**

A: The crates have very low contrast. Try turning up your in-game brightness or looking for the faint yellow circular outline on the ground. They are much smaller than standard supply crates.

**Q: Do I need to be a certain level to start the Thunder Spear quest?**

A: While there isn't a strict level requirement, it is recommended that you are at least Level 50 and have decent blade upgrades, as the Hardened Titans in Utgard are very difficult to take down with starter gear.

**Q: How many spears can I carry at once?**

A: By default, you carry 8 spears. You can increase this capacity through the equipment upgrade menu using gold and materials gathered from missions.
