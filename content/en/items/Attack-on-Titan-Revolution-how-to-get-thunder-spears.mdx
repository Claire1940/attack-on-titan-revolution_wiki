## Phase 1: Building Watchtowers in the Outskirts

The first step in your journey to unlock Thunder Spears begins in the Outskirts map. You will notice new UI elements and environmental markers that were not present in previous versions of the game.

To start the quest, you must locate **green circles** scattered around the Outskirts area. These circles indicate where a watchtower needs to be constructed. To build the tower, your character must stand perfectly still within the circle. Interestingly, this can be done in either human form or Titan form. If you are playing with friends, your progress is shared, significantly speeding up the construction time.

### The Escort Mission
Once you have successfully built three watchtowers, a horse carriage will spawn. This triggers an escort mission similar to the classic Outskirts defense modes. You must protect the carriage as it makes its way toward the Wall.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are using the Fritz family, using your "Scream" ability while in Titan form provides a localized buff to the carriage and your allies, making this defense phase much easier to manage.

Upon successfully defending the carriage to its destination, you will be rewarded with the **Thunder Spear Handle**, the first of three essential components.

## Phase 3: Scavenging the Giant Forest

The final component is often cited as the most frustrating part of learning **Attack on Titan Revolution how to get thunder spears**. You must head to the Giant Forest to find supply crates. These crates are notoriously difficult to spot because they are small, brown, and have a low-contrast yellow outline that blends into the forest floor.

### Finding the Crates
There are typically two crates spawned per match, though this may scale with the number of players in your lobby. Look for a faint yellow circle on the ground. Once you interact with a crate, you must manually carry it back to the base camp. The base camp is marked with a yellow icon on your mini-map.

1. **Scout the perimeter:** Crates often spawn near the roots of the largest trees.
2. **Check the UI:** Look for the yellow icon indicating the drop-off point once you have the crate.
3. **Teamwork:** Have one player stay at the base camp while others scout to minimize travel time.

After delivering the required number of crates (usually three in total across the team), the final part of the Thunder Spear will be unlocked.

## How to Use and Upgrade Thunder Spears

Once you have all three parts, you must navigate to the equipment menu to assemble and upgrade your new gear. It is highly recommended to max out the "Explosive Yield" and "Reload Speed" stats before taking them into a serious raid.

> **⚠️ Warning:** You cannot manually detonate Thunder Spears in the current build. You must be within a specific proximity to the target for the spear to explode upon impact.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get Thunder Spears without the Fritz family?**

A: Yes. While the Fritz family makes the quest easier due to their reworked buffs, any family can complete the objectives in Outskirts, Utgard, and the Giant Forest to unlock the gear.

**Q: Why can't I see the crates in the Giant Forest?**

A: The crates have a very subtle yellow outline and are quite small. It is recommended to turn up your graphics settings or brightness to help distinguish the crates from the ground textures.

**Q: How many Thunder Spears can I carry at once?**

A: By default, you can carry 8 Spears. Once these are used, you must find a supply station on the map to refill your stock.

**Q: Do Hardened Titans appear in every mode?**

A: Hardened Titans primarily appear in the Utgard map and during specific high-difficulty raids. If you are on the quest step for **Attack on Titan Revolution how to get thunder spears**, ensure you are playing on the correct map for them to spawn.
