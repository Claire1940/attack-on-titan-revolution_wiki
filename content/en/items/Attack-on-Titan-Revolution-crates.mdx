## Understanding the Pity System

One of the most important aspects of opening **Attack on Titan Revolution crates** is the pity counter. This mechanic prevents players from falling into an endless loop of bad luck by guaranteeing a high-rarity item after a set number of unsuccessful pulls. In the current 2026 build, the pity mechanics are more transparent than ever, allowing you to track exactly how close you are to a guaranteed Purple (Epic) or Legendary drop.

> **Important Note:** Pity counters are usually specific to the crate type. Opening 20 Scout Fashion crates will not contribute to your pity counter for Anime Allstar crates.

### Pity Thresholds and Rarity
*   **Guaranteed Epic (Purple):** Typically occurs every 30 to 40 pulls depending on the specific crate event.
*   **Guaranteed Mythic:** While much rarer, certain limited-time events provide a "Hard Pity" for items like the Spirit Wings.
*   **Reset Mechanic:** If you pull a high-rarity item naturally before hitting the threshold, the pity counter will reset to zero.

## Strategies for Opening Crates

Success in AOTR isn't just about how many crates you open, but *how* you open them. Follow these expert steps to maximize your chances of getting high-tier loot:

1.  **Save for Bulk Openings:** Opening crates in batches of 10 or 50 is generally more satisfying and helps you track your pity progress more accurately.
2.  **Monitor the Server Status:** During major updates, servers can lag. Ensure your connection is stable before opening a large number of **Attack on Titan Revolution crates** to prevent "ghost" pulls where the animation skips but the item is lost in a lag spike.
3.  **Prioritize Scout Fashion First:** If you are a new player, the Scout Fashion crates offer the most versatile "standard" gear that fits the game's aesthetic before you dive into the flashier anime crossovers.
4.  **Use Pity on High-Value Crates:** If you are close to a pity drop, switch to the crate that contains the item you want most (like the Berserk set) to ensure your guaranteed drop is something you'll actually use.
