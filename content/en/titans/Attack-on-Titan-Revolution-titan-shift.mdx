## How to Use the Attack Serum

Once you have successfully farmed the Serum from a high-level mission, you must equip it to your character's loadout. Using the **Attack on Titan Revolution titan shift** isn't just about clicking a button; it involves managing a new resource bar known as "Shifter Energy" or "Stamina."

When your energy bar is full, you can trigger the transformation. This usually involves a cinematic animation where your character sustains a self-inflicted injury to trigger the shift. In Titan form, your controls change entirely. You move from the high-flying ODM gear style to a heavy-hitting, brawler-style combat system.

### Titan Form Combat Mechanics
*   **Light Attacks:** Fast punches used to stun smaller Titans.
*   **Heavy Attacks:** Massive AOE (Area of Effect) slams that can clear groups of enemies.
*   **Special Abilities:** Each shifter form (Attack, Armored, etc.) has a unique "Ultimate" move with a long cooldown.
*   **Regeneration:** While in Titan form, you can slowly regenerate health, but doing so consumes your shift duration faster.

## Managing Your Titan Form

Once you have the power, you must use it wisely. A common mistake players make with the **Attack on Titan Revolution titan shift** is transforming too early in a mission. Instead, save your transformation for when the objective requires massive damage in a short window. For example, when a Boss Titan is stunned or when you are surrounded by more than five "Crawlers," the AOE damage of a Titan shift is invaluable.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Attack on Titan Revolution titan shift on Easy difficulty?**

A: No. Based on current game mechanics and community data for 2026, the Attack Serum only drops from missions with a difficulty setting of Hard or higher, specifically when achieving an A-grade or S-grade.

**Q: Is the Attack Serum a permanent unlock?**

A: Yes, once you have obtained and used the Serum to unlock your shifting ability, you retain that power. You do not need to farm a new Serum every time you want to transform, though there are different types of Serums for different Titan forms.

**Q: How do I increase my Titan's health?**

A: Your Titan's health scales with your character's base health and specific "Shifter" nodes in the skill tree. Equipping accessories that boost "Vitality" will also increase your durability while transformed.

**Q: What happens if I die in Titan form?**

A: If your Titan form's health reaches zero, you will be ejected from the nape in human form. You will be temporarily stunned and your shift energy will be completely depleted, leaving you vulnerable until your ODM gear can be re-engaged.
