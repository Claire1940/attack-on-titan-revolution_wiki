- Prioritize unlocking Spears as soon as your rank allows it.
- Early spear damage can feel underwhelming in higher letter grades, so treat first waves as setup.
- Don’t solo the unlock grind if your runs are failing—grouping is more efficient than stubborn resets.

### 3) Outskirts gather-and-delete pattern

- Gather clusters before committing burst.
- Use spear burst when density is highest.
- Focus on clear rhythm over flashy route changes.

### 4) Any Guard Titan rotation

Once you can transform consistently, this becomes your core loop:
- Open with Spear clears for first waves.
- Transform as soon as charge economy supports it.
- Use hardening first, then punch chain/skills.
- When cooldowns are spent and pressure spikes, use roar to buy reset space.
