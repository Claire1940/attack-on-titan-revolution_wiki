## Baron A-Grade Raid: The Colossal Challenge

To obtain the Colossal Serum and the Scorch Cloak, players must tackle the Colossal Raid on the Baron A-grade difficulty. This is currently the hardest content in [Attack on Titan Revolution](https://www.roblox.com/games/14236123211/Attack-on-Titan-Revolution), designed specifically for coordinated teams rather than solo players.

The raid is split into two distinct phases. In the first phase, your team must defend Eren while simultaneously using mounted cannons to whittle down the Colossal Titan's health. If the Colossal Titan reaches the wall or if Eren's health drops to zero, the mission fails immediately.

### Raid Strategy Steps
1.  **Divide Roles:** Assign at least two players to defend Eren from smaller Titans at the base of the wall.
2.  **Cannon Focus:** Two players should man the cannons, aiming specifically for the Colossal Titan’s chest and head.
3.  **Phase Transition:** Once the boss reaches 50% HP, the team must engage in direct combat.
4.  **Avoid Steam:** Watch for the Colossal Titan's steam emission; it deals massive damage and can easily deplete half your HP bar in seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Colossal Serum in Attack on Titan Revolution?**

A: The Colossal Serum is a rare drop from the Colossal Raid. You must play on the Baron A-grade difficulty to have a chance at obtaining it. It is recommended to go in with a full team of experienced players.

**Q: What are the best moves for the Attack on Titan Revolution attack titan?**

A: The best moveset currently includes the Berserk Mode for increased damage and the new Corkscrew Punch for high-impact single-target damage. Combining these with the "Founders Vengeance" perk provides a balanced build for both raids and missions.

**Q: Can I solo the Colossal Titan raid?**

A: While technically possible for the most elite players, it is nearly impossible for the average user. You must defend Eren and fire cannons simultaneously, which requires more than one person to manage effectively.

**Q: What do the Artifact Dice do?**

A: Artifact Dice are used to reroll or enhance the substats of your artifacts (like the Attack Scar or Armored Scar). This allows you to min-max your build by aiming for specific stat bonuses like increased range or reduced cooldowns.
