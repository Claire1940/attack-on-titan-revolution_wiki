## Prestige 3+ Transition: Guard Missions, Titan Kits, and Key Discipline

Around Prestige 3, many players lose efficiency by playing high-effort content with low structure. Instead, use a repeatable combat script.

### Guard Mission Combat Pattern (Titan-focused)

1. **Open with Spears** to thin first waves.
2. **Transform as soon as charge is available**.
3. **Cycle defensive + AoE tools first** (for wave stability).
4. **Use roar/utility during cooldown downtime** to avoid getting surrounded.
5. **Hold a good angle** so charging enemies funnel into your hitbox.

| Ability Use Priority | Purpose | Mistake to Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| **Defensive hardening/mitigation** | Survive focus pressure | Activating too late after being swarmed |
| **Slam/AoE control** | Clear clustered enemies | Using on scattered targets |
| **Gap-close punch/engage skill** | Reposition and maintain pressure | Diving too deep without cooldowns |
| **Roar/control utility** | Buy cooldown time | Spamming early with no pressure |

This part of the **attack on titan revolution best strategy** is about consistency, not flashy solo clips. Every clean run is progress; every failed run is lost time and potion value.
