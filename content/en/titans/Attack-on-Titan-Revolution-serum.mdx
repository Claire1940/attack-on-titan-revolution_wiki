## The Colossal Raid: Mechanics and Strategy

Winning the **Attack on Titan Revolution serum** requires a coordinated effort. The raid is divided into two distinct phases that test your ODM skills and your ability to manage heavy weaponry.

### Phase 1: The Wall Defense
In the first phase, the Colossal Titan slowly advances toward the wall. Your team must split into two groups:
1.  **The Defenders:** These players must stay on the ground to protect Eren from waves of smaller Titans. If Eren's health reaches zero, the mission fails immediately.
2.  **The Cannoneers:** These players must man the cannons on top of the wall. The Colossal Titan takes zero damage from blades or thunder spears in this phase. You must aim for his chest and stomach to deplete his HP to 50% before he reaches the wall.

### Phase 2: The Final Confrontation
Once the Titan reaches 50% HP, the wall is breached, and the scenery shifts. You must now fight the Colossal Titan directly. During this phase, he will utilize steam attacks and massive stomps. Players should focus on his nape whenever his steam dissipates, though be wary of the massive HP pool he possesses.

## Artifacts and the New "Scars" System

The 2026 update introduced specialized artifacts known as "Scars." Each Titan shifter now has a dedicated scar artifact that buffs their specific kit. For those who have consumed the **Attack on Titan Revolution serum**, the **Colossal Scar** is a mandatory equipment piece.

*   **Colossal Scar Stats:** Increases Colossal Titan stats by 15% and expands the **Atomic Ascension** range by 150 meters.
*   **Female Scar Stats:** Buffs psychic moves and increases roundhouse kick damage.
*   **Armored Scar Stats:** Increases "Courage" shield strength by 20%.

You can further customize these artifacts using **Ascendant Dice**. Unlike previous versions where money was the only requirement, you now need these specific dice to reroll substats for a higher chance at mythical rolls.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the drop rate for the Attack on Titan Revolution serum?**

A: The exact percentage is hidden, but it is classified as a Mythical drop from the Baron Grade Colossal Raid. Most players report needing dozens of successful runs to see one drop.

**Q: Can I trade the Colossal Serum to other players?**

A: Currently, the serum is account-bound upon receiving it from a raid drop to ensure that players earn their shifter status through gameplay. However, some cosmetic items from the raid are tradable.

**Q: How do I unlock the Baron Grade difficulty for raids?**

A: You must reach a high enough Prestige level (typically Prestige 5) and have a high Captain level to host or join Baron Grade missions.

**Q: Does the Colossal Titan take damage while in steam mode?**

A: The Colossal Titan has high damage reduction while using **Heatwave**, but he is not invincible. In the raid, you must wait for the steam to clear or use cannons to break his stance.

**Q: Are there any new mythical perks for the Attack on Titan Revolution serum users?**

A: Yes, the **Unwavering Belief** perk is a new mythical support slot item. It reduces all party member skill cooldowns by 2 seconds per kill, which is incredibly potent when the Colossal Titan is clearing entire waves of enemies.
