## Understanding the Skill Trees

Once you have learned **Attack on Titan Revolution how to get skill points**, the next step is knowing where to invest them. The game features three primary branches: Offense, Defense, and Support. A balanced build is usually best for solo players, while those in a dedicated squad might choose to specialize.

### Offense Tree
This tree focuses on increasing your damage output and the speed of your attacks. Skills here allow you to take down Aberrants and Boss Titans with fewer strikes. If your goal is to MVP every match, this is where the majority of your SP should go.

### Defense Tree
Survival is paramount in high-level missions. The Defense tree increases your health pool and reduces the damage taken when a Titan manages to grab or strike you. It also includes skills that improve your recovery time after being knocked down.

### Support Tree
Often overlooked, the Support tree is vital for long-duration missions. It improves your gas efficiency and blade durability. Without these skills, you will find yourself constantly retreating to supply crates, which lowers your overall XP-per-hour efficiency.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious when spending points early on. While some games allow for easy "respecs," it is always more efficient to plan your build path ahead of time to avoid wasting points on skills that don't fit your playstyle.
