## Mission Progression and Equipment Grade Gating

The heart of any attack on titan revolution progression guide is understanding mission gating. You don’t progress by brute force; you progress by **meeting grade thresholds efficiently**.

### Grade progression logic

- Upgrade all key equipment pieces evenly.
- Check mission requirement before spending materials.

| Progress Goal | Recommended Approach | Reason |
|---|---|---|

| Improve clear speed | Add cooldown + mobility utility first | More uptime = more kills |

### Best mission loop for account growth

1. Clear quests that refresh (daily/weekly) first.
2. Run boosted maps (luck, XP, or gold boosts) next.

4. Reinvest gains into grade and survival skills.
5. Repeat with slightly higher difficulty.

| Method | Cost Profile | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Crafting** | High gold cost | Fill specific build gaps |
| **Mission drops** | Time-based | Better for broad perk progression |
| **Shop buys** | Variable value | Only buy when the roll is exceptional |

### Prestige timing and stat choice

Prestige increases your level cap (in increments), so timing matters:
- Prestige only after preparing support resources.
- Stack scrolls/resources before reset.
- Prioritize long-term scaling choices like luck when your focus is drop-based growth.

> **💡 Tip:** Luck scaling often improves multiple systems at once (drops, farming efficiency, and account momentum), making it one of the safest long-term picks.

### Artifacts and achievement route

Artifacts are major build multipliers. Start with easy achievement tracks, then target harder achievements for top-tier artifacts later.

Recommended stat focus by playstyle:
- **Skill spam build:** cooldown reduction + utility
- **Burst build:** damage scaling + crit-oriented perks
- **Safe farmer:** survivability + sustained uptime
