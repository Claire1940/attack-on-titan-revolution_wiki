## Build for Faster Titan Kills (The Real XP Engine)

The fastest way to level is to cut downtime between kills. That means movement precision, quick target acquisition, and burst clearing tools.

### Recommended early-to-mid leveling priorities

| Skill/Perk Focus | Benefit | Leveling Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Target acquisition/lock-on utility** | Easier nape alignment | Reduces missed kill attempts |
| **Multi-target burst skill (e.g., chain/lethal style)** | Eliminates nearby Titans quickly | Greatly improves clear pace |
| **Map awareness perk (e.g., sensing Titan positions)** | Finds targets faster | Less travel, more combat time |
| **Mobility consistency** | Better ODM pathing | Faster mission rotations |

Even if your stats are average, strong targeting and routing can outperform high-stat players with poor pathing.

### Practical kill loop

1. **Spawn and scan** for the nearest Titan cluster.
2. **Pick isolated targets first** so teammates don’t steal your finishing hits.
3. **Use burst skill on grouped Titans** to chain quick eliminations.
4. **Rotate immediately** to the next cluster; don’t hover around empty areas.
5. **Maintain contribution pace** from mission start, not only near the end.

> **Warning:** If you trail behind your team too long, you may miss contribution thresholds and lose efficiency even if the mission clears quickly.
