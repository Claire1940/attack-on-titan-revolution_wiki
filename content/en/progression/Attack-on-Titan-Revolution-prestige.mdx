## The Fundamentals of Prestiging

At its core, prestiging is a "reset" mechanic common in many Roblox progression games, but it carries specific weight in this title. When you choose to prestige, you are essentially trading your current power for a higher ceiling of potential. It is not a decision to be made lightly, as it impacts almost every aspect of your character's current build.

### What Resets and What Stays?

The most common concern for players is the loss of hard-earned resources. Upon initiating a prestige, your character will return to Level 1. This means you lose access to high-level missions and must re-grind experience to regain your former standing. Furthermore, all skill points invested in your skill paths are wiped, requiring you to carefully re-allocate them as you level up again.

However, the game is generous with what you are allowed to keep. Unlike many other RPGs, your most valuable assets—specifically serums and high-tier equipment—remain in your inventory.

| Category | Status After Prestige | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Level** | **Reset to 1** | You must re-level to unlock endgame content. |
| **Coins** | **Converted** | Coins are lost but turned into **Gems**. |
| **Skill Points** | **Reset** | You regain points as you level up again. |
| **Serums** | **Kept** | Attack serums and others remain usable. |
| **Raid Drops** | **Kept** | Items like Mikasa's Scarf or Eren's Coat stay. |
| **Clans** | **Kept** | Your current clan remains active. |

> **Warning:** Before clicking the prestige button, ensure you have spent your coins on anything you might need, or be prepared for them to be converted into Gems immediately.

## The Rewards: Buff Cards and Gem Conversion

The primary motivation for engaging with the **Attack on Titan Revolution prestige** system is the immediate influx of Gems and the acquisition of Prestige Buffs.

### The Three-Card Draw
Immediately after you prestige, the game presents you with three random cards. You must choose one of these cards to serve as a permanent (or semi-permanent) buff for your new "life." These cards provide game-changing utility that can significantly alter your playstyle.

*   **Temporary Shield:** One of the most sought-after defensive buffs. This grants a shield equal to 15% of your maximum health for 20 seconds after you successfully kill a Titan. In high-density combat zones, this can make you nearly invincible.
*   **Flash Step:** An agility-based buff that enhances your mobility, allowing for quicker repositioning during intense aerial maneuvers.
*   **Offensive Boosts:** Various cards may appear that increase your critical hit chance or base damage against specific Titan types.

### Gem Conversion and the Fritz Clan
One of the most unique aspects of this system is the conversion of gold coins into Gems. Gems are the premium currency required for spins. If you are hunting for the **Mythic Clan Fritz**, prestiging is one of the most reliable ways to farm the necessary spins. The more coins you have at the time of prestige, the more Gems you receive, which directly translates into more opportunities to roll for high-tier clans.

## Pre-Prestige Checklist: Maximizing Your Gains

You should never prestige the moment you hit the level cap. To truly optimize the **Attack on Titan Revolution prestige** experience, you need to prepare your account to handle the temporary weakness of being Level 1.

### 1. Grind the Raids
Since you keep your equipment and raid drops, it is highly recommended that you farm the latest raids while you are still at your maximum power level. High-level raids are significantly harder to complete once you have reset your stats.

**Key Items to Secure:**
*   **Mikasa's Scarf:** Provides significant stat boosts that help early-level grinding.
*   **Eren's Coat:** A prestigious cosmetic that also offers utility.
*   **Attack Serums:** Essential for maintaining high DPS during the re-leveling process.
*   **Mythic Perks:** Rare drops that can be slotted into your gear to provide passive benefits.

### 2. Accumulate Wealth
Because your coins convert to gems, you should spend your final hours at max level farming as much gold as possible. This "golden hour" of farming ensures that your next prestige starts with a massive amount of spins, potentially landing you a better clan immediately.

### 3. Plan Your Skill Path
Since your skill points reset, take a screenshot or note of your current build. You might want to try a different path in your next prestige, or you might realize that your previous build was suboptimal. Having a plan for your first 20 levels will make the transition much smoother.

| Priority | Action | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High** | Farm Raids | Secure equipment that makes Level 1 easier. |
| **High** | Hoard Coins | Maximize Gem conversion for Clan spins. |
| **Medium** | Stockpile Serums | Ensure you have buffs ready for the re-leveling process. |
| **Low** | Socialize | Join a party to help boost you back to high levels quickly. |

## Advanced Strategies for Post-Prestige Leveling

Once you have taken the plunge and prestiged, your goal is to return to the endgame as quickly as possible. The **Attack on Titan Revolution prestige** cycle is most effective when done efficiently.

> **Tip:** Utilize the "Flash Step" or mobility buffs you received from your prestige cards to clear lower-level missions faster. Movement speed is often the biggest bottleneck in early-game clearing.

### Leveraging Kept Items
Because you kept your **Attack Serum** and Mythic perks, you will actually be much stronger at Level 1 than a brand-new player. Use this to your advantage by tackling "Hard" difficulty missions earlier than usual. The experience gain from harder missions will catapult you through the early levels, reducing the "downtime" associated with prestiging.

### Clan Synergy
If you managed to use your converted gems to roll a Mythic clan like Fritz, your leveling experience will be fundamentally different. Mythic clans often come with unique abilities or massive stat multipliers that trivializes mid-game content. Always check the official [Roblox Attack on Titan Revolution page](https://www.roblox.com/games/14236083946/Attack-on-Titan-Revolution) for updates on clan balances, as new patches in 2026 may shift the meta.

## The Long-term Impact of Multiple Prestiges

In Attack on Titan Revolution, prestiging isn't just a one-time event. As you stack multiple prestiges, the cumulative buffs from your chosen cards begin to turn your scout into a powerhouse. 

*   **Stacking Buffs:** While some buffs don't stack directly, the variety of utility you gain (Shields, Mobility, Damage) creates a well-rounded character.
*   **Spin Economy:** Regular prestiging is the primary "free-to-play" method for obtaining Mythic clans. By consistently converting coins to gems, you bypass the need for Robux-heavy transactions.
*   **Endgame Readiness:** Players who have prestiged multiple times generally have higher survivability in 2026's ultra-hard raid tiers compared to those who stayed at the initial level cap.

## FAQ

**Q: Will I lose my Titan Shifting abilities or Serums when I prestige?**

A: No. One of the best features of the **Attack on Titan Revolution prestige** system is that you keep all your serums. You will still be able to use your Titan shifting powers and other serum-based buffs even after resetting to Level 1.

**Q: What is the best card to pick after prestiging?**

A: This depends on your playstyle, but the **Shield buff** (15% max health for 20 seconds after a kill) is widely considered the best for survivability. If you prefer speed and skill-based dodging, **Flash Step** is an excellent alternative.

**Q: How many coins should I have before I prestige?**

A: There is no strict limit, but you should aim to have as many as possible. Since coins are converted to gems, and gems are used for clan spins, more coins directly equal a better chance at a Mythic clan. Try to finish your daily and weekly missions to max out your gold reserves before hitting the reset.

**Q: Can I prestige more than once?**

A: Yes, the system is designed to be repeatable. Each prestige allows you to further refine your character and accumulate more gems for the gacha elements of the game. Most top-tier players have prestiged several times to maximize their character's potential.
