A big part of **attack on titan revolution progression tips** is learning what *not* to buy. Gems are your flexibility currency. Treat them like progression fuel, not impulse spending money.

1. Set a gem reserve (example: keep at least 20–30% unspent).
2. Use spins in planned batches, not one-by-one panic rolls.
3. Check shop refresh windows before buying optional perks.
4. Compare battle pass value against ad-hoc store purchases.

> **Warning:** “Luck chasing” without a budget can drain your account and delay core upgrades like skills and equipment grade.

## Equipment Grade, Mission Modifiers, and Map Selection

Many players get blocked because they misunderstand mission requirements. Access usually checks **average equipment grade**, not one overleveled stat.

| System | Correct Approach | Progress Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Equipment grade** | Keep all major gear pieces climbing together | Unlocks higher mission tiers sooner |
| **Map choice** | Favor maps with XP/luck/gold boosts | Better long-term farming efficiency |
| **Modifiers** | Increase difficulty only when clear rate stays stable | Higher rewards without excessive fails |
| **Open lobbies** | Play where clear pace is reliable | Better session consistency |

### Modifier rule of thumb
Use harder settings only if your success rate remains high. A slightly lower reward with consistent completions beats high-reward wipes.

> **Tip:** Track runs per hour, not single-run payout. True progression comes from repeatable efficiency.
