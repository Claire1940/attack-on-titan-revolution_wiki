## The Core Methods of Obtaining Artifacts

In the current version of the game, players cannot simply purchase the most powerful artifacts with gold or basic currency. The Achievement system is the most direct way to earn specific artifacts. ## FAQ

**Q: Can I trade Attack on Titan Revolution artifacts with other players?**

A: Currently, most artifacts are "Bound on Pickup," meaning they are tied to your account once earned via achievements or the Battle Pass. This prevents the market from being flooded and ensures that rare gear remains a symbol of player achievement.

**Q: Do artifacts lose durability or break over time?**

A: No, unlike blades and gas canisters, artifacts are permanent additions to your inventory. Once you unlock an artifact, you can equip and unequip it as many times as you like without fear of it breaking.

**Q: Is the Premium Battle Pass necessary to get the best artifacts?**

A: While the Free Track offers several powerful **Attack on Titan Revolution artifacts**, the Premium Track usually contains the highest-rarity items and unique cosmetics. However, skill-based artifacts from the Achievement system remain some of the best in the game and are available to everyone for free.

**Q: What happens to my artifacts when a new season starts?**

A: Your artifacts are permanent. When a new Battle Pass season begins, you keep all items earned from previous seasons. The only thing that resets is the Battle Pass level itself, allowing you to start earning a new set of rewards.
