## Legendary Tier: The Pinnacle of Power

The Legendary tier represents the most powerful **Attack on Titan Revolution families**. These families are extremely rare but offer stats that can trivialize even the hardest encounters.

### Ackerman Family
The Ackerman family is widely considered the best offensive family in the game. It focuses on maximizing damage output and ODM gear efficiency.

*   **Stats:** +20% Damage, +15% Critical Chance, +15% Critical Damage.
*   **ODM Perks:** +10% Gas, +10% Range, +10% Control.
*   **Unique Skills:** 
    *   **Rage Mode:** A passive bar that fills as you deal damage; once full, it boosts all stats by 20%.
    *   **Humanity's Strongest Soldier:** Buffs all unlocked blade skills.
    *   **Let it Rip:** A spinning attack that deals massive AOE damage.
    *   **Swift Strikes:** An instant-kill move for Titans within a specific range.

### Yeager Family
The Yeager family (often referred to as Jagger in some translations) is the premier choice for players who want to focus on Titan Shifting and survivability.

*   **Stats:** High emphasis on health regeneration and shifting uptime.
*   **Unique Skills:**
    *   **Boosted Regen:** Instantly heals all injuries, restores 50% gas, and provides a full health heal.
    *   **Invigorate:** A berserk mode that grants 25% extra damage and constant health regeneration. However, it leaves the player stunned for 10 seconds after the Titan shift ends.

### Reiss Family
The Reiss family serves as the ultimate support bloodline among the **Attack on Titan Revolution families**. While it lacks the raw damage of the Ackermans, its utility is unmatched in party play.

*   **Stats:** +5% Gold and XP, -10% Upgrade Cost.
*   **Unique Skills:**
    *   **Conquer:** An AOE roar that stuns all nearby Titans for 5 seconds.
    *   **Command:** A party-wide buff that increases the stats of all allies.
    *   **Extra Perk Slot:** Allows for an additional support perk, such as Gear Master or Six Sense.

### The Power of the Zoe Family
Among the Epic **Attack on Titan Revolution families**, the Zoe family stands out due to its incredible ODM gear bonuses. With a 10% boost to both control and speed, it is the best choice for players who prefer fighting in human form. The "Last 10" skill is a high-risk, high-reward ability that increases attack by 20% and reduces damage taken by 60% for 30 seconds.

## Rare Tier: Understanding the Trade-offs

Rare families are often a mixed bag. While they provide certain buffs, they frequently come with debuffs that can hinder your performance if they don't align with your playstyle.

*   **Azumo:** The strongest Rare family. It provides +5% Damage and +5% ODM Control at the cost of -5% Titan stats. Since Titan serums are exceptionally rare, this is a fantastic early-game pick.
*   **Kirin:** Generally considered the weakest. It grants +10% Horse stats but reduces ODM speed by 5%. Given the importance of ODM gear, this trade-off is rarely worth it.
*   **Springer:** Provides +5% Health and +5% Critical Damage but sacrifices 5% Critical Chance. This is a net negative for most DPS builds.
*   **Sasha Braus:** Offers a "Potato" healing item (20% heal) but reduces Max Health by 5%. 

> **Warning:** Be careful with families like Krueger or Kirin. The penalties to Critical Chance or ODM Speed often outweigh the minor stat gains they provide.

## How to Optimize Your Family Build

1.  **Identify your playstyle:** Do you prefer staying in human form or shifting into a Titan?
2.  **Save your spins:** Do not use individual spins as soon as you get them. Wait until you have enough to guarantee a higher pity or a bulk roll session.
3.  **Check the debuffs:** Always look at what you are losing. A 5% loss in ODM speed is much more impactful than a 10% gain in gold.
4.  **Synergize with Perks:** If you have the Reiss family, use your extra support slot for "Gear Master" to compensate for the lack of innate ODM stats.

## FAQ

**Q: Which of the Attack on Titan Revolution families is the best for solo players?**

A: The Ackerman family is the best for solo players due to its massive self-buffs and high damage output. It allows you to take down Titans quickly without relying on a team for support or distractions.

**Q: Is it worth keeping a Rare family like Azumo?**

A: Yes, if you are in the early game and don't have many spins. Azumo provides a direct damage buff and better ODM control, which are the two most important stats for new players. However, you should eventually aim to replace it with an Epic or Legendary family.

**Q: How do unique family skills like "Last 10" work?**

A: Unique skills are active abilities that appear on your skill bar. For example, the Zoe family's "Last 10" grants a massive 20% attack boost for 30 seconds. The catch is that if you have any injuries when the timer ends, your character will automatically die.

**Q: Can I change my family after starting the game?**

A: Yes, you can reroll your family in the customize menu using spins. Spins can be earned by completing quests, reaching milestones, or purchasing them with Robux. Always check the current codes for free spins to help you land a top-tier family.
