## Understanding Memory Categories

Every memory in the **Attack on Titan Revolution memories tier list** falls into one of three distinct categories: **Offense**, **Defense**, or **Support**. Knowing which category a memory belongs to is the first step in optimizing your character.

### Offense Memories
These perks focus on maximizing your DPS (Damage Per Second). They typically enhance your critical hit rate, base damage, or provide buffs when you successfully strike a Titan's nape. For players focusing on "Glass Cannon" builds, filling your slots with Offense memories is the standard approach.

### Defense Memories
Defense memories are crucial for tackling Aberrant missions where a single hit can be fatal. These memories often provide health regeneration (like Life Feed), damage reduction, or resistance to debuffs. If you find yourself dying frequently in raids, transitioning to a more defensive **Attack on Titan Revolution memories tier list** selection can save your run.

### Support Memories
Support memories are often underrated but contain some of the most powerful perks in the game. These influence your ODM gas consumption, cooldowns for Shifter abilities, and overall mobility. Tactician, for instance, is a cornerstone of the Shifter meta because it allows for more frequent transformations.
