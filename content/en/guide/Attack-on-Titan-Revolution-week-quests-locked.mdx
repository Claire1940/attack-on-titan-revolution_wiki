## Why are Attack on Titan Revolution Week Quests Locked?

The quest system in Attack on Titan Revolution is divided into several categories, but the "Weekly" tab often causes the most confusion. There are standard weekly quests that refresh every seven days for all players, and then there are the Battle Pass "Week 1 through Week 8" quests.

If you see these numbered weeks greyed out or showing a lock icon, it is typically due to the seasonal schedule. The developers release these quests incrementally. For example, if the season just started, Week 2 through Week 8 will remain locked until their respective real-world dates arrive. Furthermore, while some quests are free, the most lucrative ones are tied to the Premium Battle Pass.

### Common Reasons for Locked Quests

| Reason | Description | Solution |
|---|---|---|
| **Time-Gating** | Quests for future weeks (e.g., Week 5) are not yet live. | Wait for the weekly server reset. |
| **Battle Pass Status** | Premium-tier quests require the 20,000 Gem pass. | Farm gems in the AFK zone or missions. |
| **Level Requirements** | Certain side quests only unlock at specific levels. | Grind XP in the Shiganshina or Trost maps. |
| **Equipment Grade** | High-level missions require a specific average grade. | Upgrade your ODM and Blades to at least **E+**. |

> **Warning:** Attempting to bypass the time-gate through system clock manipulation will not work, as quest unlocks are handled server-side by the developers.

## Understanding the Quest Hierarchy

To maximize your efficiency, you need to distinguish between the different types of objectives available in the game. Not every "locked" quest is a bug; often, it is a feature of the game's progression design.

### Main and Side Quests
Main quests serve as your introduction to the game mechanics and can only be completed once per account. Side quests are similar but often focus on specific NPCs or map-based objectives. If these are locked, you likely haven't completed the prerequisite mission in the chain.

### Daily and Standard Weekly Quests
These are the bread and butter of your Gem and Gold income. Standard weekly quests refresh every Sunday, providing a consistent way to earn spins. If these appear locked, check if you have already completed them for the current cycle.

### Battle Pass Weeks (1-8)
This is where most players encounter the **Attack on Titan Revolution week quests locked** issue. These quests are specifically designed to level up your Battle Pass. 

1.  **Week 1-2:** Usually available at the start of the season.
2.  **Week 3-8:** Unlock sequentially every 7 days.
3.  **Premium Rewards:** While you can see the quests, you may not be able to claim the "Premium" rewards unless you have spent the 20,000 Gems to unlock the full pass.

## How to Unlock Quests via Progression

Sometimes, the reason for finding **Attack on Titan Revolution week quests locked** isn't time, but your own character's power level. The game uses an "Average Grade" system to gatekeep content.

### The Average Grade System
Your character has an overall grade based on your ODM gear, Blades, and Scabbard. If a mission or quest requires a **Grade D**, but your Blades are **Grade F** and your ODM is **Grade C**, your average might still be too low. You must balance your upgrades to unlock higher-tier content.

| Equipment Piece | Importance for Quests | Recommended Early Grade |
|---|---|---|
| **Blades** | Determines damage output for kill-count quests. | **D-** |
| **ODM Gear** | Essential for mobility and speed-run objectives. | **E+** |
| **Scabbard** | Increases gas and blade capacity for long missions. | **E** |

## Best Skills for Quest Completion

Once you have resolved the **Attack on Titan Revolution week quests locked** status, you need to actually complete the objectives. Some quests require killing dozens of titans or surviving high-difficulty waves. Investing in the right skill tree is vital.

*   **Hand Grinder:** This is a literal life-saver. It allows you to escape when a Titan grabs you, preventing a mission failure.
*   **Hard Counter:** Enables you to escape a grab while simultaneously killing the Titan.
*   **Lethal Tempo:** One of the best offensive skills in 2026. It allows you to chain kills together, which is perfect for "Kill X Titans" weekly quests.
*   **Rising Slash:** Excellent for dealing massive AOE damage to Titans clustered together.

> **Tip:** Don't ignore the defensive side of the skill tree. Even at high levels, Titans can one-shot you if your health and defense stats are neglected.

## The Role of Prestige in Questing

As you progress through 2026, you will eventually hit the level cap. At this point, you can "Prestige." Prestiging resets your level but increases your level cap by 25 and grants powerful permanent buffs.

When you prestige, you are often given a choice between **Luck, Gold, or XP**. Experts almost always recommend choosing **Luck**. High luck stats increase the drop rate of Artifacts and the elusive **Attack Serum**, which is required for Titan Shifting. Higher luck also makes completing certain "RNG-based" side quests much easier.

## FAQ

**Q: Why are my Attack on Titan Revolution week quests locked even though I bought the Battle Pass?**

A: This is likely due to time-gating. Even with the Premium Battle Pass, Week 3, 4, and beyond only unlock as the real-world weeks pass during the season. Check the countdown timer in the Battle Pass menu.

**Q: Do I need a specific family to unlock weekly quests?**

A: No, families (like Yeager, Ackerman, or Reiss) provide combat buffs and unique skills, but they do not lock or unlock the standard weekly quest progression. Any player can access all quests regardless of their family.

**Q: Can I complete locked week quests later in the season?**

A: Yes! Once a week is "unlocked" by the server, it remains available until the end of the season. You can complete Week 1 quests during Week 8 if you wish.

**Q: What is the fastest way to increase my Grade to unlock missions?**

A: Focus on crafting and upgrading your equipment in the Equipment tab. Use the gold earned from daily quests to buy materials. Don't forget to equip **Perks**, as they provide significant stat boosts that contribute to your overall effectiveness in missions.
