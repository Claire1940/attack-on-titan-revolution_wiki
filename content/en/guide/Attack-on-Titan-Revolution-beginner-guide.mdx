## Character Customization and the Family System

Before you can even enter the training grounds, you must customize your character. The most critical aspect of this process is the **Family** tab. Your family determines the passive buffs and unique skills your character will possess, categorized by rarity.

Families are obtained through "spins" or rolls. Higher rarity families offer significantly better stat boosts and exclusive abilities that can define your playstyle.

| Family Rarity | Acquisition Method | Impact on Gameplay |
|---|---|---|
| **Common** | High roll chance | Minimal stat boosts, standard skills. |
| **Rare** | Moderate roll chance | Improved mobility or damage modifiers. |
| **Epic** | Low roll chance | Unique family-specific abilities and high buffs. |
| **Legendary** | Extremely low chance | Game-changing passives and elite skill sets. |

To obtain more spins without spending Robux, you should keep an eye on the official Discord for community codes. Developers frequently release codes during updates or milestones that grant hundreds of free spins to players.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't settle for a basic family if you have spins available. Rerolling for at least a "Rare" or "Epic" family early on will make the initial mission grind much more manageable.

## Economy Management: Gems and Spins

Gems are the primary currency in the game, used for purchasing spins, equipment upgrades, and the Battle Pass. While you can purchase gems with Robux, there are several free methods to accumulate wealth in 2026.

1.  **Quests and Achievements:** Completing daily and weekly tasks provides a steady stream of income.
2.  **Missions:** Standard missions reward gems based on performance and difficulty.
3.  **The AFK Zone:** For players who cannot actively play, the AFK Zone allows you to earn gems over time. Some advanced players use multi-instance setups to maximize this gain, though this is taxing on hardware.
4.  **Battle Pass:** Investing 20,000 gems into the Battle Pass is highly recommended, as it pays for itself through rewards and provides exclusive cosmetics and perks.

## Essential Settings for Performance

Attack on Titan Revolution is a fast-paced game where frame drops can lead to a Titan catching you mid-air. Optimization is key to survival.

*   **Graphics:** Turn off "Blur" and "Player Effects" to reduce visual clutter and lag.
*   **Shadows:** Disable shadows to improve FPS significantly.
*   **Gameplay:** If you are on a mobile device, ensure **NA Assist** is enabled to help with targeting. For PC players, turning off unnecessary invites will keep your UI clean during intense combat.

## Understanding Equipment and Missions

In this **Attack on Titan Revolution beginner guide**, it is vital to understand that your progress is gated by your **Equipment Grade**. Unlike other games where your level determines what you can do, here, your average equipment level dictates mission availability.

For example, a mission might require an **E+ Grade**. If your ODM gear is high level but your blades are low, your average grade might fall below the requirement, preventing you from starting the mission.

| Mission Difficulty | Required Grade | Primary Rewards |
|---|---|---|
| **Easy** | F to E | Basic Gems, Common Perks |
| **Normal** | E+ to D | Gold, Rare Perks, Crafting Mats |
| **Hard** | D+ to C | Epic Perks, Prestige Scrolls |
| **Insane** | B and above | Legendary Perks, Artifacts |

## Skill Tree Prioritization

The skill tree is divided into offensive, defensive, and utility branches. As a beginner, your goal should be survivability and "get out of jail free" cards. Titans in this game are unforgiving and can often one-shot players if they get a grip on them.

### Recommended Early Skills

*   **Hand Grinder:** This is mandatory. It allows you to escape a Titan's grasp when you are caught.
*   **Hardcounter:** Not only does this help you escape, but it also allows you to kill the Titan simultaneously.
*   **Lethal Tempo:** One of the best offensive skills, allowing you to chain kills together with minimal effort.
*   **Rising Slash:** Excellent for dealing massive damage to multiple Titans in close proximity.
*   **Defense Skills:** Do not ignore the right side of the tree. Increasing your health and damage resistance is the only way to survive high-difficulty missions in 2026.

> **Warning:** Avoid spending all your skill points on pure damage. A dead scout deals zero DPS. Balance your tree with at least two defensive utility skills.

## The Prestige System and Memories

Once you reach the level cap, you have the option to **Prestige**. This resets your level but increases your maximum level cap by 25 each time, allowing for more skill points and better stats.

Before hitting the prestige button, ensure you have **Prestige Scrolls**. These can be farmed in the Everend map or purchased from the Market. When you prestige, you are given a choice between boosting **Luck, Gold, or XP**.

In almost every scenario, you should **choose Luck**. Luck is the most valuable stat in Attack on Titan Revolution because it scales with everything in the game—from the rarity of perks you find to the drop rate of the elusive Attack Serum. After choosing luck, you will select "Memories," which provide permanent buffs to your new run.

## Advanced Progression: Artifacts and Titan Shifting

As you move into the mid-to-late game, your focus will shift toward Artifacts and the rare ability to shift into a Titan.

### Artifacts
Artifacts are unique items that provide specialized stat boosts. You can find them by completing specific achievements or high-level Scout feats. For instance, "The Crown" is widely considered the best artifact in the game, but it requires completing some of the hardest missions available. You should tailor your artifacts to your playstyle; if you prefer using skills frequently, focus on **Skill Cooldown Reduction**.

### Titan Shifting
To become a Titan Shifter, you need the **Attack Serum**. The drop rate for this item is notoriously low (often below 0.25%). To increase your chances:
*   Maximize your Luck stat through Prestige.
*   Run the "Attack Raid" missions specifically designed for Shifter drops.
*   Use modifiers in your lobby to increase the drop rate of rare items at the cost of higher difficulty.

A: You should prestige as soon as you hit the current level cap, provided you have at least one Prestige Scroll. Always prioritize the Luck bonus during the prestige process.
