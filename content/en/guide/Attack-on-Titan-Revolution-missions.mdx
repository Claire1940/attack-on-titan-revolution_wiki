## The Breach: Defending Shiganshina

The first major hurdle for any scout is the fall of Shiganshina. This mission recreates the Colossal Titan's attack and tasks players with defending the headquarters from an overwhelming influx of Titans. On higher difficulties like Aberrant mode, the density of Titans increases significantly, making it one of the most frantic **Attack on Titan Revolution missions** for beginners.

The primary objective is to hold the line while civilians evacuate. You will face a mix of small, medium, and large Titans, but the real threat comes from the Crawlers. These low-profile enemies move with erratic speed and can easily catch a scout off-guard if they spend too much time on the ground.

| Mission Phase | Primary Objective | Key Threat | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Breach** | Clear 5-10 Titans near the wall | Colossal Titan debris | Use high-altitude swings to avoid ground clutter. |
| **HQ Defense** | Protect the supply hub | **Crawlers** | Use "Tornado Blaze" or AoE skills to clear clusters. |
| **Final Stand** | Eliminate the remaining Abnormals | Armored Titan appearance | Focus on the nape; do not engage the Armored Titan directly. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling with the Breach mission solo, try grouping up with at least two other players. Having teammates to distract Crawlers while you go for the nape makes the Aberrant difficulty much more manageable.

## Trost District: The Struggle for Survival

The Trost storyline is divided into several sub-missions, including the Trost Scrimmage and the pivotal Boulder Defense. These **Attack on Titan Revolution missions** introduce the first Raid mechanics, where players must interact with Eren in his Attack Titan form.

In the Scrimmage, you are tasked with clearing out Titans that have flooded the city. This is an excellent time to practice your "Phantom Striker" techniques, aiming for flawless kills without taking damage. Following the scrimmage, you must defend Eren while he carries a massive boulder to seal the hole in the wall.

### Defending the Attack Titan
During the Boulder Defense, Eren moves slowly and is a magnet for every Titan on the map. You must intercept Titans before they reach him. Abnormals in this stage often sprint at "Usain Bolt" speeds, requiring you to use your gas boosts effectively to cut them off.

| Objective | Success Criteria | Failure Condition |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Escort Eren** | Eren reaches the wall breach | Eren's health reaches 0% |
| **Clear Path** | Eliminate Titans in Eren's direct line | Titans stun Eren, delaying progress |
| **Seal the Wall** | Trigger the final cutscene | Running out of blades/gas before the end |

## The 57th Expedition: Outskirts and Giant Forest

Moving beyond the walls, the 57th Expedition shifts the gameplay focus toward escorting supply carts. This is widely considered one of the most difficult **Attack on Titan Revolution missions** due to the open-field environment where ODM gear anchor points are scarce.

### Escort Mechanics
You must defend two carriages as they traverse the outskirts. Titans will spawn in waves, often targeting the horses. If a carriage is destroyed, you lose access to vital supply refills, making the rest of the mission a battle of attrition.

1.  **Conserve Blades:** Since you are far from HQ, every blade counts. Aim for "One-Tap" kills on the nape.
2.  **Use Acro Mode:** When overwhelmed by multiple Titans attacking both carts, activate your Acro/Awakening mode to clear the area quickly.
3.  **Target the Eyes:** If you cannot reach the nape in time, blind the Titan to stop its attack on the carriage.

## The Female Titan Raid: Stohess District

The climax of the first season missions involves the confrontation with the Female Titan (Annie Leonhart). Unlike standard **Attack on Titan Revolution missions**, this is a multi-stage boss fight that requires precise timing and reaction speed.

### Phase 1: The Capture
In the Giant Forest, you must defend the captured Female Titan from mindless Titans attempting to eat her. This is a reverse-defense mission where you must keep the boss "alive" (captured) while managing high-density waves. If her HP drops too low, she escapes prematurely, making the subsequent fight harder.

### Phase 2: Stohess Showdown
The final battle in Stohess involves chasing Annie through the city. She uses hardening abilities to protect her nape, meaning you must strike her joints (knees and arms) to create an opening. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Watch out for Annie's scream. It summons nearby Titans to her location, which can quickly turn a 1v1 duel into a chaotic survival situation.

| Boss Mechanic | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hardening** | Focus on unhardened joints or use heavy-hitting skills. |
| **Grab Attack** | Dash backward immediately when her hand glows. |
| **Berserk Eren** | Stay airborne; his ground slams deal massive AoE damage. |

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to deal with Crawlers in Attack on Titan Revolution missions?**

A: Crawlers are best handled by staying at a medium altitude. Wait for them to finish a lunging animation, then use a downward strike or a specialized AoE skill like "Tornado" to hit their nape while they are stationary. Avoid staying on the ground, as their hitboxes are extremely punishing.

**Q: Can I complete the Female Titan raid solo?**

A: While it is possible to solo the Female Titan on Normal difficulty, it is highly recommended to bring a team for Hard or Aberrant modes. The "Defend the Body" phase in the Giant Forest is particularly difficult to manage alone due to Titans spawning from multiple directions.

**Q: How do I unlock Aberrant mode for missions?**

A: You must first complete the mission on Hard difficulty with at least a "B" rank. Aberrant mode increases Titan health, speed, and the frequency of Abnormal spawns, but it offers the best rewards in the game.

**Q: Why do I keep running out of blades during escort missions?**

A: Blade durability is consumed based on the number of hits. To conserve them, focus on precision. Ensure your crosshair is directly on the nape before clicking. Additionally, upgrading your "Durability" stat in the skill tree is a must for long-distance **Attack on Titan Revolution missions**.
