## The Ultimate Goal: Omnipotent and the Shiki Clan

For many players, the primary motivation for hunting achievements is the **Omnipotent** achievement. This is widely considered the "Platinum" trophy of the game. It is not earned through a single action but is instead a culmination of your entire career as a scout.

To unlock Omnipotent, you must complete every other secret achievement in the game. The reward is the **Shiki Clan**, a secret and incredibly powerful lineage that provides some of the best buffs and aesthetic flares available. Because this requires the completion of the "Champion" and "Overlord" achievements, very few players will ever see this pop up on their screen.

## Mastering the Combat Grinds: Overlord & Fearless

The two most time-consuming **Attack on Titan Revolution achievements** are Overlord and Fearless. These cannot be "cheesed" or skipped; they require hundreds of hours of active gameplay.

### Reaching 50,000 Kills (Overlord)
The Overlord achievement requires approximately 50,000 Titan kills. To optimize this grind, players should focus on high-density maps like the **Outskirts** or specific raid missions where Titan spawns are consistent. 

*   **Speed is Key:** Use builds centered around high ODM gas capacity and blade durability.
*   **Difficulty Scaling:** While Hard or Aberrant modes offer more XP, for raw kill counts, playing on a difficulty where you can one-shot Titans is more efficient for the Overlord progress.

### The 25,000 Streak (Fearless)
Fearless is arguably the most stressful achievement. You must reach a 25,000 streak. This means 25,000 kills without dying or resetting. The biggest threat to this achievement isn't just the Titans—it's your internet connection.

> **⚠️ Warning:** A single disconnect or lag spike can reset your streak to zero. Ensure you are playing on a stable, wired connection and avoid peak server hours when hunting for Fearless.

## Competitive Feats: The Champion Achievement

The **Champion** achievement was a mystery for several months after the game's launch. It is now confirmed that this achievement is tied directly to the monthly leaderboards. To earn Champion, you must finish at the **Number 1 spot** on any global leaderboard at the moment the month resets.

This includes leaderboards for:
1.  Total Titan Kills
2.  Highest Streak
3.  Missions Completed
4.  Waves Cleared
5.  Raids Completed
6.  Robux Spent (for those with deep pockets)

If you are a Free-to-Play player, your best bet is to target the "Missions" or "Waves" leaderboards by dedicating a specific month to nothing but grinding those specific modes.

## Social and Hidden Miscellaneous Achievements

Not all **Attack on Titan Revolution achievements** are about killing Titans. Some require you to be in the right place at the right time or to fail spectacularly.

### The Encounter Achievement
This is a "soft-locked" achievement for many. You must be in the same lobby as **Evolution Power** (the head developer) or **EK**. These developers occasionally join public or private lobbies to test updates or interact with the community. There is no way to force this; however, joining the official [Attack on Titan Revolution Discord](https://www.roblox.com/games/14236123223/Attack-on-Titan-Revolution) can give you a heads-up when developers are active in the community.

### The Path of Failure: Immortalized
The **Immortalized** achievement requires you to die 1,000 times. While it sounds easy, it is incredibly tedious. Most players naturally reach the "Legendary" tier of deaths (250) through normal gameplay, but hitting 1,000 requires intentional "death-farming."

*   **Efficiency Tip:** Enter a mission and immediately let a Titan grab you. Repeat this process while watching a movie or doing something else. It is a test of patience rather than skill.

## Advanced Scout Feats: Supremacy & Maestro

In the Scout Feats category, two achievements often confuse newer players: **Supremacy** and **Maestro**. These require specific tactical approaches to unlock.

### Supremacy Strategy
Supremacy is often achieved through "wall cheesing." This involves positioning yourself on the interior of the wall where Titans struggle to reach you, allowing you to pick them off safely or complete objectives without taking damage. While developers have patched some of the more egregious spots, using the verticality of the map to stay out of a Titan's grab range is the intended way to show "Supremacy."

### Maestro Strategy
The Maestro achievement is best done with a partner. You need to utilize the **Lethal Tempo** skill.
1.  Enter a mission on **Easy Mode** (Outskirts is recommended).
2.  Kill two Titans quickly to activate the Lethal Tempo buffs.
3.  Have a high-level friend or "homie" clear the rest of the map while you maintain the rhythm or stay alive.
4.  This coordination ensures the achievement triggers without you risking the buff falling off.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Champion achievement on a private server?**

A: No, leaderboard positions are typically tracked through public matchmaking and official mission results. You must compete against the global player base to secure the #1 spot.

**Q: Does the Fearless streak reset if I leave the game?**

A: Generally, your streak is saved to your profile, but it is highly recommended to complete large chunks of the streak in single sessions to avoid data synchronization issues. Always use the "Return to Lobby" button rather than closing the Roblox client directly.

**Q: Is the Shiki clan guaranteed after getting Omnipotent?**

A: Yes, once the Omnipotent achievement is triggered by completing all other secret **Attack on Titan Revolution achievements**, the Shiki clan is automatically unlocked for your character.

**Q: Who counts as a developer for the Encounter achievement?**

A: Specifically, the game's owners and head developers, Evolution Power (Evo) and EK. Meeting community moderators or contributors usually does not trigger this specific secret achievement.
