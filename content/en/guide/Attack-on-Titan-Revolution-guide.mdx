Families are categorized by rarity, and you must use "spins" or "rolls" to cycle through them until you land on a lineage that fits your playstyle.

## Optimizing Your Technical Settings

High-speed ODM (Omni-Directional Mobility) combat is visually demanding. To ensure your performance remains fluid during intense Titan encounters, specific settings should be adjusted. Many players overlook the impact of visual clutter on their reaction times.

1.  **Disable Motion Blur:** This is essential for maintaining clarity while swinging through the air.
2.  **Turn Off Player Effects:** In large lobbies, other players' particles can cause significant frame drops.
3.  **Shadows:** Lowering or disabling shadows can provide a massive boost to FPS on mid-range hardware.
4.  **Aim Assist:** If you are playing on a mobile device, keep Aim Assist enabled to compensate for the touch controls.

## Equipment Grading and Skill Selection

In Attack on Titan Revolution, you cannot simply jump into the hardest missions. Every mission has a grade requirement (e.g., E+, D, C). This grade is calculated based on the **average** of all your equipment pieces, not just your blades. If your ODM damage is high but your gas capacity is low, your overall grade will suffer, potentially locking you out of higher-tier content.

### Essential Skills for Survival

The skill tree is divided into offensive and defensive branches. While it is tempting to go all-in on damage, the highest-level missions feature Titans that can "one-shot" players who haven't invested in their defense.

*   **Hand Grinder:** This is a mandatory skill that allows you to escape when a Titan successfully grabs you.
*   **Hardcounter:** A high-skill-ceiling ability that lets you escape a grab while simultaneously dealing a killing blow to the Titan.
*   **Lethal Tempo:** Widely considered one of the best offensive skills, allowing for rapid-fire kills with minimal effort once triggered.
*   **Rising Slash:** Excellent for clearing groups of Titans or dealing massive damage to the nape in a single motion.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore the "Cooldown" reduction skills. Being caught without your abilities active during a boss encounter is a quick way to end a run.

## Advanced Progression: Perks and Prestige

Once you reach the level cap, the game truly begins through the **Prestige** system. Each time you prestige, your level cap increases by 25, allowing for further stat growth. However, before initiating a prestige, you must acquire **Prestige Scrolls**, which can be found in the Market or farmed in specialized zones like Everend.

### Choosing Your Memory
When you prestige, you are given a choice between three "Memories." For the vast majority of players, choosing the **Luck** memory is the superior strategy. Luck scales with almost every drop rate in the game, making it easier to find rare artifacts, perks, and the coveted Attack Serum.

### Perk Management
Perks provide passive stat boosts and range from Common to Legendary. You can craft these using in-game currency or earn them as drops from high-difficulty missions.
*   **Common/Rare:** Good for filling slots in the early game.
*   **Epic/Legendary:** These should be your focus for endgame builds.
*   **Battle Pass:** It is often more cost-effective to spend 20,000 gems on the Battle Pass to secure high-tier perks rather than buying them individually from the rotating shop.

## Artifacts and Titan Shifting

The ultimate goal for many players is to obtain the power of the Titans. To transform, you must find the **Attack Serum**, an incredibly rare item with a base drop rate of approximately 0.24%. While this is extremely low, participating in "Attack Raids" and maximizing your Luck stat through Prestige can significantly improve your chances.

### The Crown Artifact
The **Crown** is currently the most powerful artifact in the game. To obtain it, you must complete the "Scout Feats" achievement, which is one of the most difficult challenges in the game. Artifacts should be equipped based on your specific playstyle; for example, if you prefer skill-based combat, focus on artifacts that offer Skill Cooldown Reduction.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to earn gems for this Attack on Titan Revolution guide?**

A: The most efficient method is a combination of completing your Daily and Weekly quests while utilizing the AFK Zone during your downtime. For those with powerful PCs, running multiple instances of the game in the AFK Zone can yield thousands of gems per hour.

**Q: How do I join a Regiment and why does it matter?**

A: You can join or create a Regiment for 150 Robux. Regiments are vital for competitive players because they allow you to participate in global rankings. Being in the top three for Titan kills or mission completion is the only way to earn the prestigious "Champion" achievement.

**Q: Which family is the best for beginners?**

A: While Legendary families are objectively the strongest, any family that provides a boost to ODM speed or Gold gain is excellent for beginners. Look for families that allow you to farm resources faster so you can eventually roll for a Legendary tier.

**Q: Can I reset my skill tree?**

A: Yes, skill trees can be reset, but it often requires specific items or a small gem fee. It is recommended to plan your build around either a "Glass Cannon" (pure damage) or "Tank" (defense and health) setup early on to avoid wasting resources.
