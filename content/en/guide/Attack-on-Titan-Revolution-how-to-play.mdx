## Character Creation and the Family System

Before you can step foot outside the walls, you must customize your character. The most critical element of this process is your **Family**. Families in Attack on Titan Revolution provide unique passive buffs and active skills that significantly alter your gameplay. These are categorized by rarity, and your starting family can dictate your early-game efficiency.

You obtain families by using **Spins**. While you can purchase these with Robux, savvy players utilize in-game methods to stock up.

| Family Rarity | Buff Type | Impact on Gameplay |
|---|---|---|
| **Common** | Minor Stat Boosts | Standard progression; best for early leveling. |
| **Rare** | Specialized Passives | Noticeable improvements in ODM speed or blade durability. |
| **Epic** | Unique Active Skills | Provides tactical advantages in combat scenarios. |
| **Legendary** | Game-Changing Abilities | Massive stat multipliers and exclusive combat maneuvers. |

## Mastering Attack on Titan Revolution How to Play: Economy and Resources

To progress in the game, you will need two primary currencies: **Gems** and **Spins**. Gems are used to purchase essential upgrades, battle passes, and market items, while Spins are your ticket to better family lineages.

### Efficient Gem Farming
There are several ways to accumulate Gems, but some are significantly more efficient than others:
1.  **Quests and Achievements:** Daily and weekly quests provide a steady stream of income. Achievements, located in the top right of the screen, offer massive one-time Gem rewards for milestones like "Scout Domination."
2.  **Missions:** Completing missions on higher difficulties increases your Gem yield.
3.  **The AFK Zone:** For players who cannot actively play 24/7, the AFK Zone is a viable option. Community members often use multi-instance setups to maximize their earnings while away from their desks, though this requires a robust CPU to handle the load.

## Equipment Grades and Mission Requirements

A common mistake new players make is focusing solely on a single stat, such as blade damage. However, Attack on Titan Revolution utilizes an **Average Equipment Grade** system. You cannot enter high-tier missions if your overall gear level doesn't meet the threshold.

| Mission Difficulty | Required Average Grade | Recommended Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Easy** | F to E | Basic ODM control and blade management. |
| **Normal** | E+ | Upgrading gas capacity and blade durability. |
| **Hard** | D to C | High-damage output and cooldown reduction. |
| **Insane** | B+ and Above | Specialized perks and artifact synergy. |

To improve your grade, you must consistently upgrade all components of your ODM gear. If your damage is at an E+ grade but your gas cylinders are at an F, your average may fall below the requirement for Normal missions.

## The Skill Tree: Survival and Offense

The skill tree is divided into various branches. While it is tempting to go all-in on offensive power, the high-level meta in 2026 suggests that a balanced approach is necessary because Titans can often one-shot players in higher difficulties.

### Must-Have Skills
*   **Hand Grinder:** Essential for escaping when a Titan manages to grab you.
*   **Hard Counter:** Allows you to escape a grab while simultaneously dealing lethal damage to the Titan.
*   **Rising Slash:** A high-damage area-of-effect (AoE) attack perfect for clearing groups.
*   **Lethal Tempo:** Widely considered one of the best offensive skills, allowing for rapid-fire kills with minimal effort.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore the defense side of the skill tree. Even at max level, a single mistake can result in an instant death if your defensive stats are neglected.

## Prestige and Long-Term Progression

Once you reach the level cap, you have the option to **Prestige**. Each prestige rank increases your maximum level cap by 25, allowing for further stat growth. However, prestiging resets much of your progress, so preparation is key.

Before you hit the prestige button, ensure you have a stack of **Prestige Scrolls**. These can be purchased in the Market or farmed in specialized zones like Everend. When you prestige, you are given a choice between Luck, Gold, and XP boosts. 

**Always choose Luck.** In Attack on Titan Revolution, Luck scales with almost every drop rate in the game, including the acquisition of rare perks and artifacts. After selecting Luck, you can use your memories and scrolls to further refine your permanent buffs.

## Advanced Mechanics: Perks, Artifacts, and Shifting

As you move into the mid-to-late game, your power will come from **Perks** and **Artifacts**. Perks provide significant stat boosts and are categorized from Common to Legendary. You can either craft these using in-game currency or earn them as rare drops from high-grade missions.

### Artifacts and Achievements
Artifacts are unique items that provide specialized buffs, such as skill cooldown reduction. These are often tied to specific achievements:
*   **Scout Domination:** Rewards artifacts for mastering mission completion.
*   **Technique Mastery:** One of the easier paths to mid-tier artifacts.
*   **Scout Feats:** The hardest path, leading to "The Crown," arguably the best artifact in the game.

### Titan Shifting
The most sought-after ability is the power to transform into a Titan. To do this, you need the **Attack Serum**. This item has an incredibly low base drop rate (often around 0.24% even with boosts). To increase your chances, you should focus on "Attack Raids," which are specifically designed to offer a higher drop rate for shifting serums.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way for a beginner to learn Attack on Titan Revolution how to play?**

A: Start by focusing on your daily and weekly quests to earn Gems. Use those Gems to get a decent Family through Spins, and prioritize unlocking the "Hand Grinder" and "Hard Counter" skills to improve your survivability.

**Q: How often does the Market refresh?**

A: The Market shop refreshes every 6 hours, totaling four times per day. This includes rotations for perks, cosmetics, and other essential items.

**Q: Is it worth buying perks directly from the store?**

A: Generally, no. It is much more cost-effective to save your Gems (around 20,000) to purchase the Battle Pass, which provides a more consistent stream of high-quality rewards and perks.

**Q: Can I play Attack on Titan Revolution on mobile?**

A: Yes, the game is mobile-compatible. If you are playing on a phone, make sure to enable "NA Assist" in the gameplay settings to help with targeting and movement precision.
