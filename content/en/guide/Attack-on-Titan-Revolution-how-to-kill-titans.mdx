## Core Mechanics: Speed and Timing

The most common mistake players make is trying to slash the nape while stationary or at low speeds. In Attack on Titan Revolution, damage is directly tied to your momentum. If you aren't boosting, you likely won't deal enough damage to kill a titan in a single hit, especially on higher difficulties.

### The Importance of the Boost
Always utilize your boost (typically the Space bar) when closing the distance. Speed isn't just for evasion; it is your primary source of power. When you hook onto a titan, holding the boost button ensures you maintain a sharp trajectory rather than a lazy arc.

### Timing the Click
A frequent complaint among the community is that "hitboxes are broken." In reality, the hitboxes are quite generous, but the timing is misunderstood. Because of the high speeds involved, you must click your mouse **before** your character model physically touches the titan's nape. If you wait until you are touching the skin, your momentum has already stalled, and the game may not register a successful hit.

## Advanced Maneuver: The Over-the-Shoulder Method

This is the most reliable way to handle **Attack on Titan Revolution how to kill titans** of any size. It involves using a "swerving" motion to bypass the titan's front and sides, landing you directly behind their neck.

1.  **Hook the Upper Chest:** Target the titan's upper torso or collarbone area.
2.  **Boost and Swerve:** Hold the boost key and a directional key (A or D). This causes you to swing around the titan's side.
3.  **The 45-Degree Release:** Once you reach a 45-degree angle relative to the nape, let go of the directional key while still holding the hook and boost.
4.  **The Strike:** Your character will slingshot directly toward the nape. Click early to secure the kill.

| Step | Action | Key Input |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Hook Upper Chest | Left/Right Click |
| **2** | Initiate Swerve | **Space + A or D** |
| **3** | Transition | Release **A or D** |
| **4** | Final Blow | Left Click (Early) |

## The Over-the-Head Technique

While the shoulder method is versatile, the over-the-head technique is often faster for smaller, 3-meter to 7-meter titans. This requires vertical control rather than horizontal swerving.

To perform this, hook the upper chest as usual and hold the boost. Instead of A or D, hold the **W** key. This pulls your character upward, allowing you to fly directly over the titan's head. As you pass the crown of their head, release W and click. This method is highly effective for speedrunning missions in under two minutes, though it can be inconsistent against the tallest 15-meter variants.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when using the W/S keys to adjust height. While they allow for incredible precision, holding them too long can cause you to overshoot the titan entirely, leaving you vulnerable to a grab.

No two titan encounters are identical. You must learn to adapt your hooks mid-flight. If you accidentally hook a titan's arm or lower back, do not immediately detach. You can use the W key to reel yourself upward or hold a directional key longer to "orbit" the titan until the nape becomes visible.

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I not doing enough damage to kill the titan in one hit?**

A: Damage in Attack on Titan Revolution is heavily dependent on your speed at the moment of impact. Ensure you are holding the boost (Space) and that you have equipped perks or family buffs that increase your base damage or ODM speed. If you hit the nape at a slow crawl, the titan will survive.

**Q: How do I stop getting grabbed while trying to kill titans?**

A: Grabs usually happen when you stay in the titan's "danger zone" (the front and sides) for too long. Using the **Attack on Titan Revolution how to kill titans** swerve methods described above keeps you moving quickly behind them, making it much harder for the AI to track and grab you.

**Q: Is Hook Assist considered "cheating" or "noob-friendly"?**

A: Not at all. In the 2026 competitive landscape, Hook Assist is a standard quality-of-life setting that compensates for the fast-paced nature of Roblox's engine. It allows you to focus on your positioning and timing rather than pixel-perfect cursor placement on a moving target.

**Q: Does my choice of Family affect how I kill titans?**

A: Yes. Different Families provide various stat boosts to speed, damage, and ODM gas efficiency. While the mechanical techniques remain the same, a speed-focused Family will make the "Over the Head" method much easier to execute due to the increased vertical lift.
