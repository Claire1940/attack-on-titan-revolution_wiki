## Step-by-Step Colossal Raid Route (2-Player Consistency Plan)

This section is your practical **colossal scars aotr** raid script. Run it in order.

### Phase 1 (Cannon Control + Crowd Handling)

1. **Both players open with buffs immediately.**
2. **Buffer moves to cannons** and starts damage contribution.
3. **Main player controls waves** with thunder spears and keeps map pressure stable.
4. Re-apply buffs on cooldown windows without panic spamming.

Why this works: cannon damage benefits from buff layers, so the support account can meaningfully carry phase 1 contribution even with lower personal DPS stats.

### Transition Window

- If main player has not shifted yet, rotate inside walls and prepare transform timing.
- If already shifted, farm nearby wave kills to stack momentum effects before committing to boss pressure.
- Keep the Colossal Titan away from critical objectives as long as practical.

### Phase 2 (Shifter Burst Discipline)

1. Let buffer continue crowd suppression and team buff rhythm.
2. Main player uses Female Titan skills in burst windows.
3. Avoid mindless skill dumping during boss attack animations that reduce damage value windows.
4. Re-engage after attack pattern ends; repeat cycle.

| Phase | Main Player Job | Buffer Job | Success Check |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | Crowd control + setup | Cannon DPS + buffs | Stable HP progression |
| Transition | Shift timing + stack prep | Reposition + refresh utility | No panic losses |
| **Phase 2** | Burst on valid windows | Crowd lock + buff uptime | Boss HP drops steadily |

> **Tip:** Good runs feel “calm.” If both players are constantly improvising, your timing script is probably too loose.

## Common Mistakes That Slow Down Colossal Scars Runs

Even optimized **colossal scars aotr** builds can underperform due to execution errors.

- **Overlapping identical buffs** from both players at once
- **Ignoring cannon optimization** in phase 1
- **Spamming into poor boss windows** instead of timing around attack animations
- **Dropping survivability too far** for small damage gains
- **Unclear player roles** at match start

Quick self-audit after each run:
- Did phase 1 end on schedule?
- Did support maintain buff uptime?
- Did main hold burst for valid windows?
- Did shift bar expire before final damage push?

If two or more answers are “no,” improve structure first, then tweak stats.
