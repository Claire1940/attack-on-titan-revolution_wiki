## Movement Drills to Build Air Control Fast

Great control comes from repeatable drills. Do these in short sessions before full farming routes.

### Drill 1: Air-Stay Reset (Beginner to Intermediate)

Goal: stay airborne, scan targets, and avoid random wall collisions.

1. Launch upward with your opening movement.
2. Use a backward lift pattern (quick S taps) to create distance and height control.
3. Hook nearby structures if Titan range is too far.
4. Use W when you need extra elevation to clear obstacles.

### Drill 2: Boost Rhythm (Intermediate)

Goal: increase map speed without accidental overboost.

1. Hook into a long line.
2. Tap Space in controlled intervals.
3. Add W only when you need vertical gain.
4. If you keep launching too far, slow your tap cadence.

### Drill 3: Correction Chain (Intermediate to Advanced)

Goal: recover quickly after imperfect hooks.

1. Use Hook A for entry.
2. If angle is bad, switch to Hook B correction.
3. Recenter camera on target neck.
4. Recommit only when line is stable.

| **Boost Rhythm** | **4 min** | Gas efficiency + speed | Fewer accidental overlaunches |
