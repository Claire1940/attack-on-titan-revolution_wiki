## Skill Tree Optimization: Target Accusation

Target Accusation significantly enhances your ability to track and lock onto specific Titan body parts. On a mobile screen, where finger placement can sometimes obscure your view, having the game assist in prioritizing the nape or limbs is a game-changer. ## Mastering ODM Movement on Touch Screens

The core of the **Attack on Titan Revolution mobile** experience is the Omni-Directional Mobility (ODM) gear. To move like a professional, you must master the "Flip" mechanic. Staying on the ground is a death sentence in this game; therefore, your goal should be 100% aerial uptime.

### Advanced Maneuvers

1.  **The Backflip:** Pull your joystick backward and press the "Flip" button simultaneously. This is the most important move for resetting your position in mid-air.
2.  **Side Flips:** Pull the joystick left or right while flipping to dodge incoming Titan grabs or projectiles.
3.  **The Mega Boost:** Double-tap the jump/space button while in the air. This consumes more gas but provides a massive burst of speed, perfect for escaping a Titan's reach or closing the gap on a distant target.
4.  **The "Look Down" Trick:** To ensure your backflips execute perfectly, tilt your camera slightly downward before initiating the flip. This helps the game engine register the backward trajectory more reliably on mobile devices.

| Movement Type | Input Command | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Backflip** | **Joystick Back + Flip** | Gaining distance and resetting air time. |
| **Mega Boost** | **Double Tap Space** | Rapid gap closing or emergency evasion. |
| **Lateral Dodge** | **Joystick Left/Right + Flip** | Avoiding Titan swipes and grabs. |

## Combat Strategies: Killing Titans and Raid Bosses

Killing a Titan on mobile requires a rhythm of hooking, boosting, and slashing. If your Nape Assist misses, do not panic. Redirect your hook to the Titan's leg to temporarily disable its movement. Once the Titan is slowed, use the backflip to gain altitude, line up your reticle with the nape, and use the space button to boost downward for a high-velocity kill.

When dealing with **Raid Bosses**, the strategy remains similar but requires more patience. You must utilize the flip button constantly to stay above the boss's hitbox. Wait for the Nape Assist to "red dot" the target, then engage. For bosses with eye-weaknesses, use your joystick to circle around the head, boosting in only when you have a clear line of sight to avoid being swatted out of the air.

> **💡 Tip:** Always zoom your camera out to the maximum distance. This prevents Titans from "sneaking up" on you from the side of the screen, which is a common issue for mobile players with a limited field of view.

## Thunder Spears and Titan Shifting

Thunder Spears are arguably the most powerful tools in the scout arsenal in 2026. On mobile, they can be tricky to aim, but they offer massive area-of-effect damage.

*   **Auto Detonation:** You can toggle this in the settings. For beginners on mobile, keeping Auto Detonation **ON** is recommended so you can focus on flying while the spears explode on impact.
*   **Manual Detonation:** Pro players prefer this for "nuking" specific groups of Titans, but it requires high dexterity to manage while swinging.

If you are playing as a **Titan Shifter**, remember that your attack combos matter. While many players simply mash the M1 (Light Attack) button, the **M1 -> M1 -> M2 (Heavy Attack)** combo deals significantly more damage and can guard-break enemy Titans more effectively.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Attack on Titan Revolution mobile harder than the PC version?**

A: While touch controls have a steeper learning curve, features like Nape Assist and Target Accusation are specifically designed to bridge the gap, making mobile players just as competitive in raids.

**Q: How do I fix the lag on my mobile device?**

A: Go into the game settings and lower the "GFX" or graphics quality. Additionally, ensuring your camera is zoomed out can sometimes help the device render the environment more efficiently by reducing the density of close-up textures.

**Q: What is the best skill for mobile players to unlock first?**

A: You should prioritize the Support skill tree to unlock **Target Accusation**. This skill is the single most helpful upgrade for improving your accuracy with ODM hooks on a touchscreen.

**Q: Can I use a controller for Attack on Titan Revolution mobile?**

A: Yes, the game supports Bluetooth controllers. If you find touch controls too difficult, connecting a controller will give you a console-like experience while still playing on your mobile device.
