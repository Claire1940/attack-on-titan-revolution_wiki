## Default Attack on Titan Revolution Keyboard Controls

The developers have designed the control scheme to be intuitive for long-time fans of the genre while maintaining the complexity required for a skill-based action game. By default, the game automatically detects your input method. If you are playing on PC, the keyboard and mouse layout is active the moment you load into a slot.

Below is a breakdown of the primary inputs you will use during combat and exploration.

| Action | Keybind / Input | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move Forward** | **W** | Standard character movement and forward swerving. |
| **Move Left** | **A** | Strafe left or swerve left while in the air. |
| **Move Backward** | **S** | Backpedal or slow down aerial momentum. |
| **Move Right** | **D** | Strafe right or swerve right while in the air. |
| **Left Hook** | **Q** | Fires the left grapple hook of the ODM gear. |
| **Right Hook** | **E** | Fires the right grapple hook of the ODM gear. |
| **Slash / Attack** | **Mouse 1** | Performs a standard blade strike against targets. |
| **Boost / Jump** | **Space** | Provides a burst of gas for speed or jumps from the ground. |
| **Reload Blades** | **R** | Replaces dull blades with a fresh set from your canisters. |

> **Warning:** Running out of gas or blades in the middle of a titan encounter is often fatal. Always keep an eye on your UI gauges and use the **R** key proactively during lulls in combat.

## Mastering ODM Gear Movement

The core of the experience is the ODM gear. Unlike standard movement in most Roblox games, movement here is physics-based. Using the **Attack on Titan Revolution keyboard controls** effectively means understanding how to "swerve." While you are attached to a building or titan via your hooks (**Q** and **E**), pressing the **WASD** keys allows you to influence your trajectory.

### Hook Mechanics and Dual Hooking
Firing a single hook allows for wide, swinging arcs. However, firing both hooks simultaneously (pressing **Q** and **E** together) provides a more direct, centered pull toward your target. This is essential for closing the distance on a titan's nape quickly. 

### The Art of the Swerve
Swerving is the act of using your directional keys to move around obstacles while hooked. For example, if you fire a hook into a building directly in front of you, holding **A** will cause your character to swing in a leftward arc around that building. This is vital for dodging titan projectiles or avoiding their reaching hands.

## Combat and Blade Management

Combat in the game is fast-paced and requires precise timing. Your primary interaction with titans will be through **Mouse 1** (Left Click). However, simply clicking isn't enough; you must time your slashes as you pass through the titan's hitbox, specifically the nape area for a kill.

### Blade Durability
Every time you strike a titan or hit a hard surface, your blade durability decreases. The **R** key is your lifeline. Reloading takes a few seconds, during which you are vulnerable. 

| Blade State | Effect | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Green / Full** | Maximum damage output. | Engage high-priority targets. |
| **Yellow / Worn** | Reduced damage; may require multiple hits. | Look for a safe opening to reload. |
| **Red / Broken** | Near-zero damage; highly dangerous. | **Immediate reload (R)** while retreating. |

## Advanced Settings for Keyboard Players

While the actual **Attack on Titan Revolution keyboard controls** are mostly fixed to ensure a balanced competitive environment, the "Settings" menu offers several toggles that significantly impact how those controls feel. Access the settings from the main lobby or the pause menu to fine-tune your experience.

### Camera Shake and Lock-On
For many players, the default camera shake can be disorienting during high-speed maneuvers. Lowering this setting can help you keep your eyes on the titan's nape. Additionally, the "Lock-On" feature can be toggled to help your camera stay focused on a specific target, though many pro players prefer manual camera control for better situational awareness.

### Key Optimization Tips
*   **Mouse Sensitivity:** Adjust this so you can perform a 180-degree turn comfortably without lifting your mouse.
*   **FOV (Field of View):** A higher FOV allows you to see titans approaching from your periphery, which is crucial when you are focused on a single target.

> **Tip:** If you find yourself accidentally hitting the wrong keys, try resting your fingers on the "Home Row" for gaming (Shift, A, W, D) to ensure your pinky is ready for any auxiliary shifts and your thumb is always on the **Space** bar.

## Training and Muscle Memory

Learning the **Attack on Titan Revolution keyboard controls** is only the first step. Developing the muscle memory to use them under pressure is what separates scouts from commanders.

1.  **Practice in the Lobby:** Use the non-combat zones to practice "Infinite Hooking"—staying in the air as long as possible without touching the ground.
2.  **Target Dummies:** Use the training area to practice the **Q + E** pull followed by a **Mouse 1** slash.
3.  **Swerve Drills:** Try to circle around a building 360 degrees using only one hook and the **A** or **D** keys.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change the Attack on Titan Revolution keyboard controls?**

A: Currently, the game uses a fixed layout for primary actions like hooks and slashing to maintain a level playing field. However, you can adjust camera sensitivity and secondary settings in the options menu to customize the feel of the controls.

**Q: Why are my hooks not firing when I press Q or E?**

A: Ensure you are aiming at a valid surface (buildings, trees, or titans) and that you are within the maximum range of your ODM gear. Also, check your gas meter; if you are completely out of gas, your hooks will not function.

**Q: Is it better to play with a mouse or a trackpad?**

A: It is strongly recommended to use a dedicated mouse. The **Attack on Titan Revolution keyboard controls** rely heavily on quick, precise camera movements that are nearly impossible to execute consistently on a laptop trackpad.

**Q: Does the game support macro keys?**

A: While some keyboards allow for macros, using them to automate combat or movement can be seen as an unfair advantage and may lead to account restrictions. It is best to learn the manual timing for the most rewarding experience.
