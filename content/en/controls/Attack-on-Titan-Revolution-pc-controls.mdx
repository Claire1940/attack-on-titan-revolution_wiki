| Action | Keybind | Description |
|---|---|---|

## Optimization and Settings for PC

To make the most of the **Attack on Titan Revolution pc controls**, your game must run smoothly. High latency or frame drops can cause you to miss a grapple or fail a nape strike. Follow these optimization steps to ensure your PC is ready for the 2026 update:

*   **Turn Off Blur:** Motion blur can make it difficult to track Titan movements at high speeds.
*   **Disable Player Effects:** In large lobbies or raids, other players' effects can significantly lag your CPU.
*   **Shadows:** Turn these off to boost your FPS (Frames Per Second).
*   **AFK Zone:** If you need gems for spins, use the AFK zone. Some players use multi-instance managers like BlueStacks to maximize their gem gain, though this is heavy on CPU usage.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when using third-party software to farm gems. Always prioritize the stability of your hardware and follow the game's terms of service.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change the default Attack on Titan Revolution pc controls?**

A: Yes, most keybinds can be customized within the game's settings menu. Many players prefer to move the hook keys (Q/E) to side mouse buttons for better control over their movement while using WASD.

**Q: How do I activate my family-specific skills?**

A: Family skills are usually assigned to the number keys (1-4). You must first unlock them in the skill tree and equip them in your loadout before they can be used during a mission.

**Q: Why are my hooks not attaching to buildings?**

A: Ensure you are within range and that your cursor is over a valid surface. If you are still having trouble, check your "Range" stat in the equipment tab; low-grade ODM gear has significantly shorter hook reach.

**Q: What is the best way to practice the Attack on Titan Revolution pc controls?**

A: The best way to practice is in the low-difficulty missions or the training area. Focus on "swinging" rather than just pulling yourself forward. Learning to use one hook at a time to orbit around a Titan's neck is the key to mastering the combat loop in 2026.
