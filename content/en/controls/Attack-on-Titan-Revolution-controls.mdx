## Complete List of Attack on Titan Revolution Controls

For PC players, the control scheme is designed to be intuitive yet deep, allowing for high-speed aerial maneuvers. Below is a breakdown of the primary **Attack on Titan Revolution controls** you will use during every mission.

| Action | Keybind | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Left Hook** | **Q** | Fires the left grapple hook to anchor onto buildings or Titans. |
| **Right Hook** | **E** | Fires the right grapple hook for directional maneuvering. |
| **Dual Hooks** | **Space (Hold)** | Fires both hooks simultaneously for a direct forward pull. |
| **Gas Boost** | **Space (Double Tap)** | Consumes gas to provide a sudden burst of speed in mid-air. |
| **Primary Attack** | **Mouse 1** | Performs a standard blade slash; timing is critical for neck hits. |
| **Skill 1 / 2 / 3** | **1, 2, 3** | Activates equipped skills like Rising Slash or Lethal Tempo. |
| **Open World Lobby** | **Tab** | Switches to the social lobby to interact with other players. |
| **Interaction** | **V** | Used for interacting with NPCs or picking up items. |
| **Reload Blades** | **R** | Replaces broken or dull blades with a fresh set from your canisters. |

> **💡 Tip:** To maintain maximum momentum, avoid holding both hooks for too long. Alternating between **Q** and **E** allows for sharper turns and more unpredictable movement patterns.

## Advanced Combat Skills and Execution

Once you have mastered basic movement using the **Attack on Titan Revolution controls**, you must integrate specialized skills into your repertoire. The skill tree is divided into offensive and defensive branches, both of which are vital for high-difficulty missions.

### Essential Skills to Unlock
The following skills significantly alter how you interact with the game's mechanics:

1.  **Hand Grinder:** This is a life-saving defensive control. If a Titan grabs you, activating this skill allows you to escape their clutches before being eaten.
2.  **Hardcounter:** A high-level maneuver that lets you escape a grab while simultaneously dealing a counter-attack to kill the Titan.
3.  **Lethal Tempo:** Widely considered one of the best offensive skills in the game. It allows you to chain kills across three Titans with minimal effort if timed correctly.
4.  **Rising Slash:** An excellent Area of Effect (AOE) attack that deals massive damage to multiple targets in close proximity.

## Optimizing Settings for Performance

Precision in **Attack on Titan Revolution controls** is often hindered by technical lag or visual clutter. To ensure your inputs are registered instantly, adjusting your graphical and gameplay settings is highly recommended.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| **Motion Blur** | **Off** | Increases clarity during high-speed ODM maneuvers. |
| **Player Effects** | **Off** | Reduces CPU/GPU load in crowded lobbies or missions. |
| **Shadows** | **Off** | Significant FPS boost for lower-end hardware. |
| **NA Assist** | **On (Mobile Only)** | Essential for mobile players to lock onto Titan napes easily. |
| **Invites** | **Personal Preference** | Turn off to avoid distractions during intense combat. |

By refining these settings, you ensure that the **Attack on Titan Revolution controls** feel responsive, which is critical when you are attempting to dodge a Titan's lunge at the last millisecond.

## The Role of Families and Perks

*   **Common Families:** Provide minor buffs to XP or Gold.

Perks further customize your playstyle. These range from **Common** to **Legendary** and can be crafted or found as mission drops. If you find yourself struggling with the timing of the **Attack on Titan Revolution controls**, look for perks that increase your "Perfect Dodge" window or reduce skill cooldowns.

## Titan Shifting and Serum Mechanics

For players who want to step beyond human limitations, Titan Shifting is the ultimate goal. However, this requires the **Attack Serum**, an incredibly rare item with a base drop rate as low as **0.24%**.

To increase your chances of obtaining the serum:
1.  **Stack Luck Boosts:** Use Prestige points to invest in Luck, as it scales with every drop in the game.
2.  **Equip Artifacts:** Items like the **Crown** (earned through Scout Feats) provide significant stat enhancements.
3.  **Play Higher Difficulties:** Harder missions have a slightly better yield for rare items.

Once you become a Titan Shifter, the **Attack on Titan Revolution controls** shift to a melee-focused combat system, emphasizing heavy hits and regenerative abilities rather than ODM mobility.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change the default Attack on Titan Revolution controls?**

A: Currently, most keybinds are fixed, but you can adjust sensitivity and specific gameplay toggles in the settings menu to better suit your playstyle.

**Q: What is the best way to practice the ODM gear controls?**

A: The open-world lobby is a safe environment to practice swinging. Focus on "cornering"—using a single hook to swing around a building—to build muscle memory before heading into missions.

**Q: Why are my hooks not attaching to the Titans?**

A: Ensure you are within range. The ODM gear has a maximum tether distance. Additionally, check your "Lock-on" settings in the gameplay menu, as this can affect how your **Attack on Titan Revolution controls** prioritize targets.

**Q: How do I get more spins for families?**

A: You can earn spins by completing daily and weekly quests, using codes provided by the developers on Discord, or by purchasing them with gems earned in the AFK Zone.
