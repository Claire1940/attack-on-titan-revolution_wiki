## The Fundamental Reload Mechanic

In Attack on Titan Revolution, your blades are not permanent. Every strike against a Titan's nape or limbs reduces the durability of your current set. Once the durability meter hits zero, your blades will shatter, or "dull," significantly reducing your damage and speed.

To perform a reload, you must have spare blades available in your Omni-Directional Mobility (ODM) gear sheaths. By default, the input for this action is the **'R' key** on your keyboard. When you press this, your character will discard the current blades and slide a fresh pair into the handles. This animation takes a few seconds, during which you are unable to attack, making timing the most critical factor in combat.

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Reload Blades** | **R** | **X / Square** | Discards dull blades for fresh ones |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not wait until your blades are completely broken to reload. Dull blades have a massive damage penalty, often requiring three times as many hits to secure a kill.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I reload my sword in Attack on Titan Revolution?**

A: There are two main reasons you might be unable to reload. First, check if you have spare blade sets remaining in your inventory; if you are out of blades, the 'R' key will do nothing. Second, ensure you are not currently in the middle of another animation, such as being grabbed by a Titan or using a special skill.

**Q: How do I get more spare blades for my gear?**

A: You can increase your spare blade capacity by upgrading your sheaths at the Blacksmith or by equipping higher-tier ODM gear. Some specific perks in the skill tree also allow you to carry extra sets.

**Q: Does reloading my sword cost money or gold?**

A: No, the act of reloading during a mission does not cost currency. However, you must "purchase" or equip the blade sets as part of your loadout before the mission starts. Replenishing blades at a supply crate during a match is also free of charge.

**Q: What is the fastest way to master Attack on Titan Revolution how to reload sword?**

A: The best way to master the reload is to practice "Air Reloading." Practice grappling onto a building, jumping, and pressing 'R' while in mid-air. This keeps you mobile and ensures that by the time you land or reach your next Titan, your blades are already fresh and ready for combat.
